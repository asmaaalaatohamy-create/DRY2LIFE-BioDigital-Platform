# 📚 Scientific Research References

The decision-making rules, PGPR bacterial dosages, and crop yield estimations used in the **DRY2LIFE** platform are strictly derived from and calibrated against validated Egyptian agricultural research papers.

## 📑 Core References

### 1. El-Akhdar et al. (2025)
*   **Key Finding:** Application of multi-strain Plant Growth-Promoting Rhizobacteria (PGPR) resulted in up to a **197% increase in grain yield** under salinity stress conditions.
*   **Application in Platform:** Calibrates the optimized bio-remediation dosage recommendations for Moderate and High salinity zones.

### 2. Zaki et al. (2025)
*   **Key Finding:** Highlighted a **70% reduction in salinity impact** and a **115% increase in root length** when introducing specific halotolerant bacterial strains.
*   **Application in Platform:** Validates the visual simulations of root growth and recovery curves in the interactive charts.

### 3. Fouad et al. (2026)
*   **Key Finding:** Documented a **92% chlorophyll retention rate** in wheat crops subjected to high salinity stress up to 15 dS/m when treated with specialized bio-agents.
*   **Application in Platform:** Sets the baseline safety thresholds for the smart alert system.
